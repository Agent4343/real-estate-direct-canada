# Document Submission and Email Requirements Guide

## üìß Email Submission - Legal Requirements

### Can Documents Be Submitted Via Email?

**YES** - Email submissions are legally acceptable in Canada, BUT with important requirements:

1. **Email Must Contain Essential Terms**
   - Property details
   - Price
   - Parties involved
   - Clear intention to be bound

2. **Email Signature Requirements**
   - Typed name can constitute signature
   - Must show clear intent
   - All parties must acknowledge

3. **Provincial Variations**
   - Some provinces require formal written agreements
   - Electronic signatures generally accepted
   - Final agreements may need formal execution

4. **CASL Compliance**
   - Must obtain consent for commercial emails
   - Include identification information
   - Provide unsubscribe mechanism

#### British Columbia (BC):

#### Ontario (ON):
- Agreement of Purchase and Sale
- Property Disclosure Statement
- Tarion Warranty (if new build)
- Latent Defects Disclosure

#### Alberta (AB):
- Property Disclosure Statement
- Real Property Report
- Condominium Documents (if applicable)
